Ground Rules

  1. Never skip a day. Consistency compounds faster than intensity.
  2. Apply while studying. Start from Day 3 onwards.
  3. Build while learning. Reading alone is not enough.
  4. Know your architecture deeply. Explain every component confidently.
  5. Speak concepts out loud daily. Interviews reward clarity.
